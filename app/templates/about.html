Background

It is often said that Western countries are becoming increasingly polarised societies, with this polarisation occuring around politically-charged issues like climate change and immigration.

Simulating social phenomena

One way to understand a phenomenon of interest is through simulation. To run a simulation involves making some good guesses about the processes that are central to that phenomenon in order to recreate that same phenomenon at a smaller scale. This can be done computationally, although it need not be.
